SenseBoard MK1

Features

The keyboard of your dreams. Smooth switches, long battery life, analog input, immense customizability, gamepad emulation, native Home Assistant integration via Matter and much, much more

The Sense-Switches are specifically designed for smoothness and a good feel under each finger, with a 25g bottom out force optimised for all-day typing without fatigue.

The keyboard is designed with the user in mind. It shouldis designed to be a beautiful product that is a joy to see on your desk

The SenseBoard MK1 have 5 built in 18650 cells with a total capacity of 16 000 mAh. This means a battery life of about a month of daily use with RGB and up to five months without lighting

No proprietary software

The Senseboard MK1 does not have its own application that takes up processing power on your comuter, instead it tavively integrates with microsoft dynamic lighting for the RGB effects. And because the keyboard has a native Home assistant integration it means that you can configure the keyboard to connect to your self hosted smart home and enabling you to use the keyboard as a control centre and allows you to control all your prefered smart home entities

Future Updates

Because the keyboard is designed to integrate with your smart home the keyboard will update automatically when a new update arrives and the device is connected to home assistant. If you don't use or have home assistant you can always update to the latest version directly from our downloads page. Note: I am a single developer maintining this project at the time and therefore some bugs will arise in the firmware. Any help and feedback is appreciated and an archive of updates to fall back to if the keyboard were to become unusable can be found on my GitHub
